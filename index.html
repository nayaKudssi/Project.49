Interesting Facts about Chameleons

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels are large land mammals best known for their humps
  • The camel's hump is its most notable feature
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff
  • She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Cubs are well protected in their first month

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadidae can be found on every continent except Antarctica
  • Cicadas can't eat or bite because they lack jaws
  • Cicadas drink a lot and pee a lot of fluids
